About Stories by Memories

Stories by Memories is a professional photography brand dedicated to capturing real emotions and unforgettable moments. Our goal is to turn your special occasions into beautiful visual stories that you can cherish for a lifetime.

We specialize in capturing natural, candid, and artistic photographs that reflect the true essence of every event. With creativity, passion, and attention to detail, we ensure that every picture tells a story worth remembering.

Whether it is a wedding, celebration, portrait session, or a special milestone, we focus on preserving those moments with elegance and authenticity.

Meet The Photographer

Pramod

Pramod – Founder & Lead Photographer

Pramod is the creative mind behind Stories by Memories. With a deep passion for storytelling through photography, he believes that every moment holds an emotion worth capturing.

Over the years, Pramod has developed a unique style that blends candid moments with artistic composition. His work focuses on capturing real emotions, natural expressions, and timeless memories that couples and families can cherish forever.

For Pramod, photography is not just about taking pictures — it is about preserving stories. Every wedding, celebration, and portrait session is approached with creativity, dedication, and attention to detail.
